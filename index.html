Neurology Services

Neurology Consultation

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment of acute and chronic neurological disorders using evidence-based international guidelines.

Migraine & Headache Clinic

Diagnosis and treatment of migraine, chronic headache, cluster headache, tension headache and trigeminal neuralgia.

Stroke Care

Acute stroke evaluation, secondary stroke prevention, rehabilitation planning and long-term follow-up.

Epilepsy Clinic

Diagnosis and medical management of epilepsy, first seizure, difficult-to-control epilepsy and seizure disorders.

Parkinson's Disease Clinic

Evaluation and treatment of Parkinson's disease, atypical parkinsonism, tremors, dystonia and other movement disorders.

Vertigo & Balance Disorders

Assessment and management of dizziness, vertigo, vestibular migraine and balance disorders.

NCS / EMG

Nerve Conduction Studies (NCS) and Electromyography (EMG) for neuropathies, carpal tunnel syndrome, radiculopathy, plexopathy, myopathy and motor neuron disorders.

EEG

Electroencephalography (EEG) for seizure disorders, epilepsy and other neurological conditions where brain electrical activity assessment is required.
